Welcome to the **Student Practice Sandbox (Practice Mode)**!

In this mode, you will conduct independent time-of-flight measurements on different specimen metals.
1. Select a material, specimen thickness, and testing frequency.
2. Review the plotting canvas to identify matching wave peaks.
3. Input your peak choices in the controls panel on the left and submit your attempt.
4. Click **Start Practice** to begin. 

The Research Assistant (RA) and Principal Investigator (PI) will grade your submission programmatically, classifying any physical anomalies or peak mis-selections to help you learn!
